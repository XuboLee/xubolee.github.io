# About Me
I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the [Department of EIC](https://ei.hust.edu.cn/) at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, working with Prof. [Yong Xiao](https://sites.google.com/site/xyong2007/home).

My research interests include network intelligence, agentic AI networks, wireless sensing, and machine learning for communications.
